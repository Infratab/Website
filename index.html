Know us

Infratab is an Internet-of-Things company providing a Quality Monitoring platform for the perishables* industries. Our purpose at Infratab is ambitious yet simple: to measure Freshness and provide our customers the tools to make better decisions about their inventory.

*perishables are items like fruits, flowers, dairy, produce, fish, meat, vaccines, adhesives etc…

Our Freshtime suite of Sensors, Software & Analytics together help our customers in making better decisions in efficiently handling their perishables supply chains to reduce spoilage, protect and improve their brand’s identity.
